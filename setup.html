Setup · Form ID extractor

Google Forms identifies each field with a hidden entry.XXXXXXXXX number. To make the auto-fill work we need to map each form field to its ID. You only do this once — and it works even when you don't own the form.

METHOD ABookmarklet (easiest)

  1. Drag this button to your browser's bookmarks bar:

    📋 MLA · Extract IDs
  2. Open the form in a new tab: click here
  3. Once it loads, click the bookmarklet you just saved
  4. A panel will appear with every field + its entry ID. Click "Copy mapping"
  5. Come back here and paste it in the box at the bottom

METHOD BView page source

  1. Open the form in your browser
  2. Right-click anywhere on the page → View Page Source (or Ctrl+U)
  3. Press Ctrl+F and search for FB_PUBLIC_LOAD_DATA_
  4. Select that whole long line (it's huge — starts with var FB_PUBLIC_LOAD_DATA_ = [...])
  5. Paste it in the box below — the tool will parse out every entry ID + label
